Frequently Asked Questions

Why enkab web design?

At enkab web design, our top priority is to create clean, high-performing websites that align with our clients' business goals.
Most of our clients do not need complex AI systems or bloated CMS platforms.
That said, we build all our sites from scratch, focusing on solving business problems while optimising for site speed, accessibility, and SEO best practices to ensure strong search engine performance.
All delivered at competitive prices.

How do you ensure my business's visibility is improved?

At enkab web design, we do not leave our clients to figure things out on their own, as we understand this can be quite daunting.
From the day your website is launched, we constantly monitor its performance and make improvements where necessary.
Because clients have varying needs, this service is completely optional after your included support period.

What does the maintainance package include?

Our maintenance packages vary depending on the complexity of your site. For simpler sites, this includes monthly content updates, basic functionality fixes, performance optimisation, and basic search engine optimisation (SEO). This package starts at £49/month. Prices will vary for more complex websites.

What is quality assurance?

Quality assurance (QA) is the process of ensuring that our clients' websites meet high technical and visual standards. Since quality is our top priority, QA is included in all our web design and development packages at no extra cost.

Is a content management system part of the package?

No.
Most of our clients do not require frequent content updates, so we avoid unnecessary platforms to improve site speed and performance.
However, for clients who require a CMS, we set up a simple, lightweight CMS that doesn't compromise on website performance.
This can be added to your package for an additional fee.
