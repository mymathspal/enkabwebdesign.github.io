Frequently Asked Questions

Why enkab web design?

At enkab web design, are top priority is to create clean and high performing websites that aligns with our client's business goals.
Most of our clients do not need the next AI-powered ... or a bloated CMS.
That said, we build all our sites from scratch, optimising for speed and accessibility and making sure our websites our optimised well to rank high on search engines.
All at competitive prices.

How do you ensure my business's visibility is improved?

At enkab web design, we do not leave our clients to figure out stuffs themselves as we understand this can be quite daunting.
So from the day your website is launched, we will constantly monitor the performance of site to improve where neccessary.
Clients have varying needs hence this is completely optional after the 30-day free trial.

What does the maintainance package include?

The maintenance package for enkab web design varies based on the complexity of the site.
For simpler sites, this includes monthly content updates, basic functionality fixes, performance optimisation and basic search engine optimisation(SEO). This package starts at £49.
Once again, prices vary based on the complexity of the website.

What is quality assurance?

Quality assurance is the process of ensuring that our clients' websites meet high standards of quality. Since quality is one of our top priorities, quality assurance is included in all our web design and development packages.

Is a content management system part of the package?

No.
Most of our clients do not require frequent content updates so we avoid bloating the site which improves the site's perfomance exponentailly.
However, for websites that require frequent content updates, we set up a simple light-weight CMS that do not compromise on the website's performance.
This is done at an additional fee.
